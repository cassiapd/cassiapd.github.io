Welcome to my website where I review yummy brunches I have had!

Mapua's best Muesli



This muesli is from Alberta's. It is called the Quinoa and Buckwheat granola and is served with coconut yoghurt, seasonal fruits and a mylk of choice. It costs $16.20. Personally I'm not a big fan of coconut yoghurt and muesli but the fruit and presentation was great! My family visit in the middle of winter but the sun was still shining, I was still chilly though. There are lovely views of the wharf and Rabbit Island. This is a popular cafe for people to bring their friendly dogs.

A freezing Açai in Napier

A freezing Açai in Napier



This delicious açai bowl is from Hapī Clean Kai Co-op in Hasting St in Napier. It costs $14 which in my opinion, is a good price for an organic smoothie bowl. I like the fact that all their packaging is compostable and that they actually do compost it, but they did not accept our container for my smoothie bowl. The seating is quite cramped as it only one high bench facing the wall so it's not good for large groups. The decor was nice but it does not get the morining sun so it was quite cold.

My first Vegan croissant

My first Vegan croissant



This incredible croissant is from Nicoletta's in Miramar, Wellington. This is the first croissant I have had since going vegan and I even went to a French speaking country. The croissant cost $6 it is rich but not very filling. Personally the atmosphere of the cafe was ice but a bit chilly and not many people were there. But overall a great selection of local unique vegan eats.

Another açai - but by the beach!

Another açai - but by the beach!



I'm very picky about acai's, I like them a certain way and this was not one of them. I was already annoyed as we were on the main strip of cafe's at Mt Maunganui Beach and it was a pretty cold day. We had gone into multiple cafes and nothing served vegan options but my family was set on this one place so we asked, seeing as they couldn't accommodate my food requirements. I asked if I could bring food from another cafe along the strip. After looking all over the only place that had vegan options is Swell. The price of the acai bowl was very expensive and it had the worst dried fruit - goji berries. Also it was not acai weather.

An Ohope muesli

An Ohope muesli



This muesli is from the Moxi Cafe in Ohope. It was a basic muesli with coconut yoghurt (we already know this isn't a fav of mine) and is towards the upper end of the price range. The environment was very nice, the cafe is in an old container and they can change the roof depending on the weather. It was a light friendly environment. The actual muesli wasn't that nice and neither was the drink I ordered. A green juice in plastic :( with chunks of kale in it.

Facon pancakes at the Mount!

Facon pancakes at the Mount!



This is the best breakfast I have had in a long time. I have really wanted to go to this cafe for a while, and I finally convinced my Mum for us to have breakfast here. We went in to the cafe and Mum was not convinced. It was down a side street an quite gloomy. Just then the waitress mentions all the tables in the courtyard are avaliable. The sun was pouring into the garden on the beautiful morning so a 10/10 for setting. The pancakes themselves were quite rich but very delicious with a generous amount of Maple Syrup (which I often find is an issue with pancakes).
